Willing to Unlearn and Relearn?

With India moving steadily towards competency-based education, educators must upskill themselves to stay relevant and employable. If you have the time and commitment to deepen your conceptual knowledge and hone your applied thinking, if you are a strong believer in equitable and inclusive education, if your focus is on 'how to learn' rather than only 'what to learn', if your happiness lies in sharing than hoarding and if your success is in passing on your skills to the future generations, we will train you to shift from a culture of rote memorisation as learning to achievement of mastery in competencies.
To be the Torchbearers as envisaged by the National Education Policy 2020 at PICT Model School, look through the subject-specific prerequisites given below.

Procedure

    Step 1: Fill in the application form with the required details (Note: all the fields need to be filled in)

    Step 2: Scrutiny of application form and verification of documents. Resume with current photo, self attested photocopies of all relevant documents need to be attached with the application form.

    Step 3: Taking a written assessment comprising three parts: General Aptitude, Teaching Aptitude and Subject Specific Assessment.

    Step 4: Upon scoring 80% and above, a demo will be scheduled on a mutually agreed timing.

    Step 5: Based on the success of the demo, a final interview with the Principal will be scheduled to discuss the job profile, terms and conditions of employment and remuneration.

    Step 6: Offer letter will be issued to selected candidates. Candidates have to send a written acceptance to confirm their employment within 24 hours from the date of issue.

    Step 7: Appointment letter will be issued on the first day of joining PICT Model School. If a candidate fails to report to duty on the first day without prior notification, the appointment will stand cancelled thereof.

List of Documents Required

  1. School Leaving Certificate
  2. Pass Certificate & Mark Sheet of Grade 12
  3. Pass Certificate & Mark Sheet of Graduation / Highest examination passed
  4. Pass Certificate & Mark Sheet of B.Ed.
  5. Pass Certificate & Mark Sheet of statutory training for Language Teaching, if applicable
  6. Appointment order / document stating the current remuneration and post held
  7. PAN Card
  8. Aadhar card
  9. Residence Proof (if residing in own house else rent agreement)
  10. Relieving Order from the current organization
  11. Testimonials from previous employers
  12. Any other relevant document

We value time. Therefore, prior appointment is appreciated. Candidates have to carry one set of self-attested photocopy of the above mentioned documents. Originals will be required for verification. The entire procedure may take at least half a day.
