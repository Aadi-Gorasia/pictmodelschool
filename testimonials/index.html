Dear Madam
It is a privilege to have our child go to PICT. It was exemplified by the amount of planning and preparation that had gone into and behind the scenes. More importantly, balancing safety and enjoyment is quite a tightrope and I must acknowledge that this was perfectly balanced by each one of the staff at PICT. There were times when children were quite tired of the unending briefings and there was a lot of commotion in WhatsApp groups expressing angst and displeasure, but with the advantage of hindsight, I would be the first to admit that each act by the school, each diktat and each guideline issued, were singularly responsible to make the trip a safe environment for the learners.
The choice of location and the depth of information given to the learners is also commendable. Without rambling further, I would like to end this note with a deep sense of appreciation and also urge you to ignore any snide comments or negative feedback from any quarter because the only thing that I can say with certainty, is that a trip of such a magnitude couldn’t have been possible without a very determined set of folks at the helm providing unwavering direction.
