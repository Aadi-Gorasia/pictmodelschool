Our Achievements

Celebrating the remarkable accomplishments of our students and faculty in academics, arts, sports, and beyond.

Best Delegation Award: DTMUN 2024

In a showcase of exceptional leadership and dedication, PICT Model School was honored with the prestigious Best School Delegation Award at the Dynamic Training Model United Nations (DTMUN) 2024, the largest and most competitive MUN in Pune.

  • Best Delegate: Tia Marathe, Neil Kayarkar, Krish Gupta
  • High Commendation: Kartik Bang, Anaysha Borade
  • 7+ Special & Honourary Mentions across multiple committees.

Excellence at BITS Pilani YEB Bootcamp

Our students excelled at the Young Entrepreneurs' Bootcamp by BITS Pilani, an immersive program designed to forge the next generation of job creators. Their innovation and diligence stood out among 144 participants.

  • Best Journal Award: Anvi Joshi, Naitik Sarnot, Aayushi Mantri (3 of only 6 winners!)
  • Prestigious Internship Secured: Debashmee Mondal & Jiya Khandelwal with Khageshvara Aviation Technology.
