Our School

Learning by Doing

PICT Model School has adopted a unique methodology that is well suited to the child's innate quest to learn by "doing". Most of the learning activities are through a number of sequential and scientifically designed tools for multi-sensory aspects and individualized education. The school provides an array of valuable experiences from which children create their knowledge.
