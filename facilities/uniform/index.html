Uniform

The school has designed the uniform after rounds of discussion and deliberation with reputed uniform vendors – Vardi Makers.

The fabric selected has a perfect blend of polyester and cotton threads to make it comfortable for the child's physical activities. The basic fabric with proper use does not require heavy ironing which makes it easier for daily use.

The colours have been selected based on the school logo colours keeping the darker colours to areas which may get dirty the quickest. In the long run, this will facilitate the ease of maintenance. The styling takes into consideration the formal look it must carry in schools and to ensure the practicality for physical activities
