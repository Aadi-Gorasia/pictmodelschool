Your Child's Safe Passage, Our Promise.

Every school day begins and ends with a journey. We are dedicated to making that journey as safe, comfortable, and reliable as the education we provide within our walls.

Our Safety Commitments

School Bus Ridership

For students availing school transport, these rules ensure a disciplined and secure environment for all.

  • Carry ID card at all times.
  • Arrive at the stop 5 minutes early.
  • Remain seated with seatbelts fastened.
  • Present the Escort Card for pickup.

Own Transport Protocol

For parents providing their own transportation, these guidelines help maintain safety and smooth traffic flow on campus.

  • Strict drop-off/pickup timings apply.
  • No double parking to avoid congestion.
  • Adhere to the 10 km/hr campus speed limit.
  • Cooperate respectfully with security staff.
